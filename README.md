### Project Description

**A powerful, end-to-end Power BI dashboard** that transforms raw Blinkit sales data into clear, strategic business intelligence for India’s leading quick-commerce platform.

This project analyzes sales performance, outlet operations, product trends, and customer behavior across different city tiers and outlet types. Every step — from data cleaning and modeling to advanced DAX and stunning visualizations — was designed and developed by me from scratch.

The dashboard helps stakeholders make faster, data-driven decisions to drive revenue growth, optimize inventory, and improve merchant performance in the fast-paced 10-minute delivery industry.

### Key Features

- Executive KPI Cards: Total Sales, Average Sales per Transaction, Total Items Sold, Average Customer Rating
- In-depth Outlet Analysis by Type, Size & Location Tier (Tier 1/2/3)
- Product Category Insights (Dairy, Fruits & Vegetables, Soft Drinks, Hard Drinks, etc.)
- Fully Dynamic Matrix – Switch between Sales, Quantity, Avg Sales & Rating using slicers
- Advanced Interactivity: Cross-filtering, Drill-through, Bookmarks & Reset All Filters
- Clean & Professional UI/UX Design with mobile responsiveness
